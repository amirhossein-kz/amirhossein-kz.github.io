Autonomous Vehicle Environment Perception

  • Github: Link
  • Result: Ranked 1st Team in the National Rahneshan Competitions for Autonomous Vehicles

Environment Perception is a crucial asset when it comes to Autonomous Vehicles. The system is required to perceive several entities in its field of view. In this project, we designed and coded an environmental perception system for an autonomous vehicle; applications of this system are as below:

  1. Traffic signs and lights detection
  2. Vehicle detection
  3. Pedestrians detection
  4. Sidewalks detection
  5. Crosswalks detection
  6. Road lanes detection
  7. Distance measurement
In this work, we utilize various computer vision methods and algorithms to fulfill the sought-after task.
