Autonomous Car Simulation Based on AVIS Engine (FIRA Worldcup 2021)

  • Github: Link
  • Results:
    • Ranked 2nd Team in FIRA Competitions in Autonomous Cars League (Race Section)
    • Ranked 3nd Team in FIRA Competitions in Autonomous Cars League (Urban Section)

We participated in the FIRA 2021 Worldcup. This competition was composed of 2 stages:

  • Race competition
  • Urban competition
For both, we utilized computer vision methods to detect the lane and traffic signs. In addition, we used ultrasonic sensors to avoid collision with obstacles. Then, we tried to keep the car in the right lane by using a digital PID controller. Furthermore, the use of behavioral cloning in the research and development process has also been investigated.
